(EN):
Weapon pack imported directly from the game "Blockade 3D Classic". 
There is no melee weapon in this version, 
but as soon as it becomes possible to add it, I will release a separate pack. 
In the current form, the weapons have not undergone much changes on my part, 
as I tried to keep a touch of the originality of those weapons that were presented in the game.

As for the 3D weapons from the "blockade", I do not plan to port them yet.


The pack was developed for the mod version: 1.1.1f   ||  MCglTF is not required.
